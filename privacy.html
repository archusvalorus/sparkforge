SPARKFORGE

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

Developer: Hearth & Hammer, LLC

Overview

Sparkforge is an arena survival roguelite game developed by Hearth & Hammer, LLC. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how our app works.

Data We Collect

Sparkforge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to play the game.

Third-Party Services

Sparkforge uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — serves optional rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — processes in-app purchases (Remove Ads). Purchase data is handled entirely by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Sparkforge displays optional rewarded video advertisements. Ads are never forced — they appear only when you choose to watch them for in-game benefits (revive or upgrade reroll). Purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase eliminates all advertisements while retaining the gameplay benefits.

In-App Purchases

Sparkforge offers a single one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. This purchase is processed by Apple and does not involve any data collection by us.

Data Storage

Game progress (high scores, run statistics) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Sparkforge does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require or request any personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hearthandhammer.ai.
