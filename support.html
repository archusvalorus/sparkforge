About the Game

Sparkforge is an arena survival roguelite where you control a glowing spark fighting off waves of enemies in a dark forge arena. Collect upgrade cards, build synergies across 6 elemental tags, and see how long you can survive. Runs last 2–3 minutes. Death is inevitable — but how far you get is up to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move?

Touch and drag anywhere on the left side of the screen. A floating joystick appears at your touch point. Release to stop. Your spark attacks enemies automatically.

What are the upgrade cards?

When you level up, you choose 1 of 3 upgrade cards. Cards belong to 6 elemental tags (Fire, Shock, Bleed, Guard, Void, Chill) plus Neutral cards. Collecting multiple cards of the same tag unlocks powerful synergy bonuses at 3, 5, and 7 stacks.

What does "Reignite the Forge" do?

After dying, you can watch a short video ad to revive and continue your run. You get a brief period of invulnerability after reviving. This can only be used once per run.

What does "Reforge Cards" do?

During the upgrade card selection screen, you can watch a short video ad to reroll all 3 cards and get a new set of options. This can only be used once per run.

What does the Remove Ads purchase include?

The one-time Remove Ads purchase eliminates all video advertisements from the game. You still get the revive and card reroll features — they just become free with no ad required.

How do I restore my purchase?

On the title screen, tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will verify your purchase with Apple and re-enable the ad-free experience.

Is the game fair without spending money?

Yes. Sparkforge is designed to be fully enjoyable without watching any ads or making any purchases. Ads are always optional and only appear at natural pause points — never during gameplay.

My game data was lost. Can you recover it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your save data and cannot recover it if lost. Uninstalling the app will erase your high scores and run statistics.
