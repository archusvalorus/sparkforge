## B. Tuning

### B1. Arena 1 pacing +smidge
- "Kind of easy — stay in place and machine gun." Dial up slightly;
  the post-boss swarm is delightful and stays untouched.
- Candidates (tune at build): spawn interval floor, melee thinning
  chance, ranged spawn chance/time. Small moves only.

### B2. Ashling shard spawn protection (Arena 2)
- Live-build find: AoE splash from the parent's death kills shards
  instantly — undermines the splitter mechanic.
- Brandon's preferred shape (stylized, not a raw i-frame): parent
  dies → shake/explode beat → THEN shards pop up, vulnerable once
  visible. The delay IS the protection; no unfair insta-trio-kill,
  no invuln jank.
- Build note: spawnAshlingShards currently fires synchronously in
  onEnemyKilled; needs a short scripted beat (~0.4–0.6s) between
  parent death VFX and shard physics existing.

## C. Forge XP Coins (new boss-death mechanic)

- KEEP: boss death XP-orb shower (works brilliantly).
- NEW: boss also erupts FORGE XP COINS — large spinning orange coins
  that scatter ACROSS THE ENTIRE ARENA (not clustered at the corpse).
  Players scoop bonus forge XP while fighting the post-boss swarm —
  keeps the endgame loop rewarding and gives a reason to push on.
- Build notes:
  - New pickup type → claims physics bit `0x1 << 9`?? NO — next free
    bit is `0x1 << 8` (CLAUDE.md); claim it and update the Portfolio
    Registry physics ladder when built.
  - Coin visual: large, spinning (xScale oscillation reads as spin),
    ember-orange — MUST not collide with pickup color canon (orange =
    player/forge lane, which is exactly right for forge XP; distinct
    from blue magnet, green health, small XP orbs).
  - Scatter: arena-wide random distribution with despawn timer
    (tune); collected → pendingForgeXP/addForgeXP pipeline.
  - Interaction to decide at kickoff: do coins respect the XP Boost
    (2x forge XP) ad? Do magnet orbs pull them? (Proposed: no magnet
    — walking to them is the point, same philosophy as health orbs.)

## E. Remove Ads package

### E1. PRICE IS WRONG — flagged ASAP by Brandon
- Title shows "$2.99"; actual product price is **$3.99**.
- ⚠️ SCOPING TENSION: Brandon said "ASAP" but also "everything folds
  into v1.8." A mislabeled price is a trust/compliance-adjacent issue
  (label under-states, charge sheet shows truth at purchase). Options
  for kickoff: (a) fast v1.7.1 with just this one-line fix, or
  (b) v1.8 Unit 0, first thing built.
- THE REAL FIX: stop hardcoding — pull the localized price string
  from the StoreKit product (IAPManager already fetches products).
  Never wrong again, works in every storefront currency.

### E2. Relocate + restyle the button
- Distinct box/rectangle, pushed to the BOTTOM of the title screen,
  away from the incoming menu/codex/bestiary access points (see A).
  Goal: zero accidental taps.

### E3. Value-proposition popup on tap
- After tapping Remove Ads, show a modal explaining the deal BEFORE
  the purchase sheet: all ads removed, but every earned reward stays —
  revive, reroll, extra card, extra pick, blessing choice, XP boost
  all become free taps. ("Remove all ads — keep every reward. Just
  tap and you're rewarded.") The value prop is invisible until
  players have met the rewarded placements; this fixes that.

### E4. In-run "No Ads" affordance
- Tiny logo-type icon on the game screen, out of the field of play.
- ⚠️ LAYOUT COLLISION: Brandon proposed upper-right — the pause
  button lives there (and its thumb-size hit zone). Resolve at
  kickoff: upper-LEFT (above buff badges), or pause-menu placement,
  or upper-right with pause relocated. Do not shrink the pause target
  we just fixed in v1.7 Unit 0.
