# Sparkforge v2.0 — "Arena 5 + The Mote" (Outline)

*Outline drafted July 15, 2026 during v1.9 planning. NOT yet a full kickoff —
this is the shape so we build v1.9 (systems) knowing where content lands. The
**walk-away** version: after this ships, Sparkforge is in a polished,
comfortable place to set down for a few months. Full kickoff + DoDs when v1.9
is close to done.*

**Thesis:** The five-arena vision completes, and the Codex's "something is
missing" finally answers. Clearing **Arena 5** triggers a single, tightly
coupled beat: the **Mote entrance** — a scripted surprise that one-shots you
("wait, wtf just happened?") — immediately followed by the **earned Spark
skin** as the reward. Plus **Boss Mode** (all five bosses now exist) and a
tuning pass. The finale.

## The coupled core (ships together — do not split)

The whole point is one emotional beat: **Arena 5 clear → Mote one-shots you →
you earn the skin.** Shock into reward. These three are one unit of experience:

1. **Arena 5** — the fifth arena (arena / enemies / boss / **tier-aware
   cards**, per the Lyra brief handed off during v1.9). Its clear is the
   trigger for everything below. Larger open plane; identity per Lyra.
2. **The Mote entrance** — a scripted moment, a **level-5-style bonus for
   existing players**: "Mote the Terror" appears and one-shots the player — the
   deliberate "why did I get one-shotted, what WAS that?" hook. Pays off the
   reserved Codex `????` slot + `docs/mote-v2.0-handoff.md` (VERY purple +
   neon-white reveal). Perception/dread, not a fair fight (yet).
3. **Earned Spark skin** — fires immediately after the Mote one-shot. A
   **reusable cosmetic framework** (skin registry + owned/unlock persistence +
   apply-on-Spark, extensible for future skins) + the first skin: evolves the
   base Spark (v1.8's two eyes → focused/anime eyes, flame detail, particles).

## Separable (the cut-line)

4. **Boss Mode** — [[boss-mode-vision]]: a bosses-only mode, extensible
   auto-registering pool, modes (randomized / canonical-from-Slag-Titan /
   theme). Enabled by Arena 5 completing the five-boss set. **This is the
   natural v2.0 cut-line** — nothing else depends on it; slips to v2.1 if the
   sprint strains, protecting the comfortable-walk-away goal.

5. **Refinement + tuning pass** — a polish/tuning sweep across the game as the
   set-down point. Optionally: the **5-boss tuning pass** (tune all five as a
   set) if Boss Mode ships here.

## Dependencies / sequencing
- **Arena 5's cards are tier-aware** → depends on v1.9's card-leveling engine
  shipping first. (Why v1.9 is systems-first.)
- **Lyra Arena 5 brief** handed off during v1.9 (`docs/lyra-arena5-brief.md`),
  so content is ready to build when v1.9 ships.
- **Breadth / Generalist 7th-tree synergy** (completes the 7×3 Codex grid) —
  candidate for v2.0 if scope allows, else v2.1. Fills the greyed Neutral row.
