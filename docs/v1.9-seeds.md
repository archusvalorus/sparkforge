## Theme candidate: "run-power feel" — make raw progression satisfying

Several banked ideas cluster around *how good leveling up feels moment to
moment*. If v1.9 wants a spine, this is a strong one.

### Seed A — Two-tier level-ups (Brandon, July 14 2026)

**The idea:** a level-up becomes a **two-tier choice**, not one card pick:

1. **Tier 1 — utility pick:** choose a stat bump (e.g. +HP / +ATK / +DEF).
   Player agency over their *foundation*.
2. **Tier 2 — ability card:** the current card-selection system, unchanged.

**Why this over flat auto-values (the first framing):** auto +5 HP / +1 ATK /
+1 DEF every level would already make each level-up land a dopamine hit even
when the offered card is situational — but making it a **choice** is better:
it decouples "what stats do I want" from "what ability showed up (card RNG),"
gives every level two meaningful beats, and **opens future build possibilities**
(an HP-foundation vs ATK-foundation run identity independent of which cards
appear). Brandon's call: choose, don't auto-grant.

**Why NOT v1.8:** this re-tunes the entire run power curve — per-level bumps
compound hard (a small +ATK/level is +20 by level 20), rippling into enemy
HP/damage scaling, every card's relative value, boss lethality per arena, and
the Forge Path budget. It's a full-version balance concern and a heavy
per-level playtest pass. v1.8 is already overloaded (Mirrorwound + Codex +
synergy rework + Forge Path screen), and layering a core-curve change on top of
the fresh Unit 5b balance would confound it. **Let 5b ship and settle, then
tune the level-up curve against a stable baseline.**

**Build notes for when it's scoped:**
- Make increments `GameConfig`-driven (one-file tuning).
- Apply via a single `applyLevelUpBonus()` path; retune enemy scaling per arena.
- Open design questions: sequencing (stat pick before or after the card?);
  does Tier 1 offer all three or a choice of 2-of-3?; magnitude + whether it's
  flat or slightly diminishing; how it coexists with the Forge Path's stat role
  (the meta-progression stat picks) so the two don't feel redundant.

### Seed B — Stat-boost card vein (banked, seeds §F)

Natural companion to Seed A — a family of cards oriented around raw stat
growth. Together, A + B could anchor the "run-power feel" theme.
