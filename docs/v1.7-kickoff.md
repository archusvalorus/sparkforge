# Sparkforge v1.7 — "The Coilworks" Sprint Kickoff

*Scoped July 9, 2026, same-day as v1.6 submission. Brandon's scope
philosophy update: the old descoping instinct compensated for handoff
tax that no longer exists — more can fold into each release, but DoD
gates between units stay absolute, and the cut-first list exists from
day one. Supersedes v1.7-kickoff-draft.md.*

**Thesis:** The game learns to sing. Sound arrives, the spark earns its
place at the center of the screen, the UI grows up (pause, settings,
buffs, build inspection), and the third arena teaches routing.

## Decided — do not relitigate

- **Sound is IN** — scaffolding for Suno-generated BGM (Brandon is
  starting a sub). SFX now; BGM toggle placeholder in settings now;
  tracks arrive when they arrive.
- **Analytics persistence is IN.** Eating our veggies.
- **Cinder Halo RETURNS** — halo restyled to float ABOVE the head
  (more halo-y, per Brandon), static or slow-drift, never a ring
  around the body. "Ornament, not armor" satisfied.
- **Rotating full-block Braceguard stays benched** → arena 4 or 5 elite.
- **Daily Forge ad is IN** — free random blessing stays whole; the ad
  buys AGENCY (choose your blessing instead of random). Needs a new
  AdMob unit (Brandon console task) before wiring.
- **Boss stage-clear sweep: REJECTED, permanently.** Leftover enemies
  during a boss entrance are the "oh shit" moment. Canon.
- **Pairing canon holds:** Coilworks (arena) + Forge Paths (system).
  If the month strains, Forge Paths slips to v1.8 — cut-first item.

## Unit sequence

### Unit 0 — Foundations quickie
- **Pause button fix:** hit zone is `y < 60` but the button draws at
  y≈80 from top — the tap target is above the visible button. Align
  and enlarge to thumb size.
- **Analytics persistence:** JSON to disk, capped ring buffer.
**DoD:** pause works every tap, on device; runs survive app restart in analytics.

### Unit 1 — Sound & Settings scaffold
- AudioToolbox SFX: orb pickups, card select, boss entrance, player
  damage, level-up, build-hint chime. Volume-appropriate, never shrill.
- Settings storage: `sfxEnabled`, `bgmEnabled` (UserDefaults; BGM is a
  placeholder toggle until Suno tracks land).
**DoD:** SFX audible and mute-able mid-run; toggles persist across launches.

### Unit 2 — Pause Menu v2
- New pause screen: RESUME / SETTINGS / MENU.
- Settings pane: sound + BGM toggles (from Unit 1).
- **Build viewer:** the cards you've picked this run, shown as mini
  tree-tinted cards (or icon+name rows) with the synergy tiers you've
  hit. "What buffs do I have again?" answered in one tap.
**DoD:** pause → inspect build → resume flows cleanly; settings apply live.

### Unit 3 — Buff Badges (HUD)
- Replace the tiny colored dots with per-tree BADGES on the left edge:
  tag icon (🔥🩸❄️⚡🕳️🛡️) + count, card-style treatment (translucent
  tree-tinted plate, colored stroke), larger text.
- One badge per tree, count = cards picked in that tree; synergy-tier
  reached could brighten the badge (t3/t5/t7 = brighter stroke).
**DoD:** legible at arm's length mid-swarm; updates on every pick.

### Unit 4 — Spark Glow-Up + asset pipeline
- Phase A (procedural-plus, no assets): layered animated core (inner
  white-hot, mid ember, outer glow), directional ember trail while
  moving, level-up flare that scales with level.
- Phase B (optional, Lyra-gated): swap to textured sprite via
  `Assets.xcassets` texture atlas behind the same PlayerNode API —
  pipeline established even if v1.7 ships Phase A only.
- Lyra call (July 9): ship Phase A; texture atlas is v1.8/v2.0 polish
  unless already cheap. Art direction in `lyra-response-v1.7.md`
  ("a coal learning to become a star" — no face, ember lane is
  player-only, trail = flecks not smoke, pickups never recolor).
**DoD:** the spark looks like the hero of every screenshot; 60fps held.

### Unit 6 — Forge Paths (meta-system) ⚠️ cut-first if month strains

### Unit 7 — The Coilworks (arena)
- ArenaConfig entry (palette per Lyra: `#121315` floor, `#5B4A22`

**DoD:** third arena selectable, visually distinct, gated correctly.
